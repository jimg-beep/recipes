For the spicy tomato sauce

300ml passata

2 small chilli, finely chopped

2 tbsp soy sauce

2 tbsp maple syrup

1 tbsp oil

1/2 tsp cornstarch (diluted in 3 tbsp water)

To make the spicy tomato sauce

Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Once hot, fry the chilli for one minute.

Reduce the heat and add the passata. Bring the heat back to medium and cook for about five minutes.

Pour in the soy sauce and maple syrup, then cook for three more minutes, stirring regularly.

Add the diluted cornstarch and mix to combine. Cook for about two minutes or until the mixture has thickened.
